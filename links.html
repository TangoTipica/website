Our inspiration

  • Zotto & Milena
    Miguel Angel Zotto and Milena Plebs, living legends in an iconic performance of Gallo Ciego.
  • Zotto and Daiana
    Zotto, the greatest living dancer, extremely well matched by Daiana Guspero, in a dazzling Milonga performance.
  • Pepito & Suzuki
    Maestros of the Milonga, unsurpassed dancers and great teachers.
  • Natacha Poberaj & Jesus Velasquezs
    Choreographed but beautifully authentic style!
  • Claudia & Esteban
    Inspiring teachers who combine authentic style and true glamour.
  • Marisa & Oliver
    Great teachers who gave us invaluable insight into poise and movement.
  • Tango and Chaos in Buenos Aires
    A treasure trove of material on the great milongueros - recommended reading!
  • Carel Kraayenhof y su Sexteto Canyengue
    Contemporary tango composer and orchestra recreating the style of Pugliese.
  • Michal Segal, Alexander Technique teacher
    Alexander Technique, a great way to improve tango posture.

Tango Tipica supports local charity Volunteer Tutor Organisation Glasgow (VTO) (charity No SC000403), which provides tutoring for children needing educational support.
