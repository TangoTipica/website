Our vision

The essence of Tango for us is the expression of musicality in harmony with the dance partner. Tango is about the unique, in-the-moment experience of being as a dance couple completely attuned to each other.

Our Tango is intimate and restrained, reliving the authentic 1940s atmosphere of the Buenos Aires milongas and Orquesta Tipicas. Authenticity and integrity are important. We seek to continuously perfect technique, musicality and style, and encourage authentic improvisation.

With our classes and workshops we want to open up Tango to everyone - the old Tango maestros were ordinary people living ordinary lives who lived for the special moment of moving across the dance floor as "one body with four legs".

     Catherine Daniela Dogaer and Wim Taghon
