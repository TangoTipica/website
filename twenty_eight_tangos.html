28 Tangos: February Tango Challenge

Tango Tipica Glasgow invites you to take part in our "28 Tangos" project - do something tango-related every day in February and share your experience via Twitter (#28tangos) or our 28 Tangos Facebook page.

You can post a video or photo of yourself dancing, share a tango track you have listened to or a video which you like or a book, a tango poem...

Anyone can take part in the 28 Tangos project but you must you adhere to the rules:

1. No promotion/advertising for any products or services, tango or otherwise.
2. No negative comments/flaming of any kind.
3. You can link to third-party content but you must add value by indicating why you post the link. Make sure you don't violate copyright. Posts violating these rules will be deleted and the user will be blocked.

We were inspired by "28 Drawings Later: February Drawing Challenge" a hugely popular project by Victoria Evans, so please Like Victoria's page.
