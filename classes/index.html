Tango Classes in Glasgow

Last few places remaining for series starting Fri 11 September.

Vintage 1940s Tango classes in Glasgow with Catherine Daniela Dogaer and Wim Taghon.

Discover the true Authentic Argentinian Tango as it was danced in the Salons of Buenos Aires in the Golden Age.

In the Beginners classes you will learn classic basic Tango steps such as the forward ocho, combinations, embellishments, floor craft, interpreting the music and easy improvisations. Once you master the basics and you move on to a more advanced level, you will learn complex Tango moves and be encouraged to develop your own style.

Book now for the Authentic Argentinian Tango Beginners classes starting Friday 11 September in Glasgow Westend. Last few places remaining.
Catherine & Wim make Tango accessible to people of all ages and abilities and get quick results. Read what their pupils are saying.

Dates & Times

The Authentic Argentinian Tango Beginners series consists of 9 classes on Fridays 6.15pm-7.15pm, starting 11 September (no class on 25 Sept & 30 Oct). Last few places remaining.

Why our Tango classes are unique

  • Professionally run and expertly taught classes. Catherine Daniela Dogaer and Wim Taghon have been dancing the Authentic Tango for 18 years and teaching in Glasgow for 11 years — the longest running Tango classes in Glasgow.
  • Everyone enrols with a dance partner so no one needs to sit out. No compulsory partner rotation.
  • Catherine & Wim received many years of formal training by established, world-class Tango teachers.
  • They dance and teach the Authentic Argentinian Tango as it was danced in Buenos Aires in the 1940s — unique in Scotland.
  • They teach in small groups so every dance couple gets personal attention.
  • Over the years Catherine & Wim have developed a unique teaching methodology which is varied and engaging, and will help you fulfil your potential in a fun and relaxed way.

Think you can't dance?

You think you're not the dancing type? So did we until we discovered the Argentinian Tango. To dance the Tango, you don't need that elusive 'natural sense of rhythm'.
Argentinian Tango is a restrained, stylish improvisation dance – steps can be combined in endless variations.
You're warned, once you're dancing, it's a passion for life!

  • You can dance Tango all over the world.
  • Authentic Tango stimulates creativity.
  • Our classes help you to improve your posture.
  • Dancing Tango can increase your confidence.
