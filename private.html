Private tuition

We are available for private classes for intermediate and advanced dancers. If you are a beginner or post-beginner (less than six months dancing experience), you will benefit most from our group classes.

In a private session, we can focus on improving your style and posture or on expanding your repertoire of steps. Private classes are taught by both Catherine and Wim.

Dance partners

In a private session, we teach one or more dance couples. You need to book with a dance partner.

Location

We teach preferrably in the West End of Glasgow, either at our regular teaching location or at your location.

Prices

Private classes are per hour and are taught by both Catherine and Wim. Prices depend on the duration of the session as well as the location. Please contact us.
