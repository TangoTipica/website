The sample is from 'Clavel Rojo', a tango composed by Carel Kraayenhof and played by Carel Kraayenhof y su Sexteto Canyengue. This internationally renowned sexteto from the Netherlands plays in the style of Osvaldo Pugliese and Astor Piazzolla.
