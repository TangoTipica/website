Colophon:

  • Drawings by Wim using Adobe Illustrator and a Wacom tablet.
  • "El Último Café" is a tango by C. Castillo & H. Stamponi.
  • The drawings of the boat are based on scenes from "Le Lotus Bleu" by Hergé (Casterman).
  • The drawing of Buenos Aires is based on a photograph taken from "Les femmes de Corto Maltese" by Hugo Pratt & Michel Pierre (Casterman).
  • The bandoneon player is based on a photograph of Anibal Troilo, from the El Bandoneon CD "Cuando tallan los recuerdos".
