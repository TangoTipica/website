Mission

We are passionate about keeping the Authentic Argentinian Tango alive and sharing the joy of dancing Tango as a couple. At the same time it is important to us to support a local good cause. Glasgow-based charity With Kids provides individual, social and practical support to children and families in Scotland.

Our professional activities give us the freedom to focus on sharing the skill we have acquired over many years of intense training and practice with world-class teachers. This freedom allows us to continue teaching Tango purely for the pleasure of sharing the Authentic Tango experience with our students and renewing our own our appreciation of the Authentic Tango.

Our professional experience in course design, teaching and customer experience design also helps us to create a truly rewarding experience for our students. Thanks to this we have over the years built up a dedicated group of Advanced/Master level students who return year after year.
