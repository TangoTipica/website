Our teaching approach

We have over 8 years experience teaching Authentic tango at all levels, from Beginners through to Advanced. We teach regular classes in Glasgow Westend as well as workshops by invitation.

Over the years we have built up a loyal group of advanced dancers who return to refine their technique and take their musicality to masters level.

Classes

In our classes, we work with small groups (max. 10 dance couples for Beginners) so we can give you our full attention.

We offer classes as a series of sessions so that in each class we can build upon the previous one and you learn quicker. During each series we organise a few dance evenings so that you can practise, experiment and discover new combinations.

Especially for beginners, we focus on learning you what you need to enjoy a night's dancing at a milonga (tango dance evening).

For all levels, we guide you through a new move 'step-by-step' and explain how you can make new combinations. Most people – even if they have been dancing for years – find this enlightening. We also concentrate on style, posture, improvisation and on how to adapt your dancing to different musical styles.

Workshops

Advanced dancers come to us to improve their sense of musicality and rhythm, to perfect their technique and to master the finer points of leading and following.

We teach the authentic tango style with close embrace as it was danced in the Salons of Buenos Aires in the 1940s – rare in the UK, unique in Scotland.

We specialise in Tango de Salon, Milonga and Tango Vals.
